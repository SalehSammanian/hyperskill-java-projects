# Simple Tic-Tac-Toe
A simple player vs. player, in terminal tic-tac-toe game.

## Example: 
````
---------
| _ _ _ |
| _ _ _ |
| _ _ _ |
---------
Enter the coordinates: 
> 1 3
---------
| X _ _ |
| _ _ _ |
| _ _ _ |
---------
Enter the coordinates: 
> 2 3
---------
| X O _ |
| _ _ _ |
| _ _ _ |
---------
Enter the coordinates: 
> 2 2
---------
| X O _ |
| _ X _ |
| _ _ _ |
---------
Enter the coordinates: 
> 1 3
This cell is occupied! Choose another one!
Enter the coordinates: 
> 1 1
---------
| X O _ |
| _ X _ |
| O _ _ |
---------
Enter the coordinates: 
> 3 1
---------
| X O _ |
| _ X _ |
| O _ X |
---------
X wins
````
